Key segments from the CIMP rules:
FWB/version (1.1=identifier, 1.2=separator, 1.3=version)
AWB Consignment Details (2.1=AWB number, 2.2=origin/dest, 2.3=quantity, 2.4=volume, 2.5=density)
FLT - Flight Bookings (3.1=FLT, 3.2=carrier+flight, 3.2.5=day)
RTG - Routing (4.1=RTG, 4.2=first destination and carrier, 4.3=onward routing)
SHP - Shipper details (5.1=SHP, 5.4=name, 5.5=address, 5.6=location, 5.7=coded location, 5.8=contact)
CNE - Consignee information (structured similarly to shipper)
AGT - Agent (7.1=AGT, 7.2=account, 7.4=name, 7.5=place)
SSR - Special service requests with text descriptions
NFY - Also notify parties
ACC - Accounting information (10.1=ACC, 10.2=accounting details)
CVD - Charge declarations including currency, charge codes, prepaid/collect indicators, carriage value, customs value, and insurance
RTD - Rate description with charge line counts, pieces, weight, rate class, commodity, chargeable weight, rate per charge, and totals
OTH - Other miscellaneous information
PPD - Prepaid charge summary covering weight charges, valuation, taxes, and charges due to agent or carrier
COL - Collect charge summary
CER - Shipper's certification with signature
ISU - Carrier's execution with date, place, and authorization
OSI - Other service information
CDC - Credit card charges in destination country
REF - Sender reference including office address, file reference, and participant details
COR - Customs origin with code
COM - Commission information
SII - Sales incentive information
ARD - Agent reference data
SPH - Special handling details with codes
NOM - Nominated handling party
SRI - Shipment reference information
OPI - Other participant information
OCI - Other customs, security and regulatory control information with country, info identifier, customs identifier, and supplement details
